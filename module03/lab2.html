Fortnite dances, also known as emotes, have become a cultural phenomenon since the game’s rise to popularity. These quirky, energetic moves are performed by characters within the game, often inspired by real-world dance trends, viral videos, or pop culture references. Players can unlock or purchase these dances, using them to express themselves during gameplay or after a victory. Some of the most famous Fortnite dances, like the "Floss" or "Orange Justice," have transcended the game itself, appearing in social media trends, schoolyards, and even in professional sports events. The catchy moves and fun animations have made these emotes an iconic part of Fortnite’s overall appeal, adding an extra layer of enjoyment to the game.
